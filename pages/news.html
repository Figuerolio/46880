Several years ago, Goldy revealed that the tribute band was planning to start working on a project of original material, and debuted three of the new songs during their appearance in May 2016 at the motorcycle rally and concert 'Ride For Ronnie' in Encino . He also affirmed that he, together with his partner Jeff Pilson, worked for the debut album of Dio Disciples. It was in 1986 when he joined Dio, during the tour "Sacred Heart" after the original guitarist Vivian Campbell was fired from the band after a controversial commercial disagreement with the singer and Ronnie's manager.
